The Story

Started with a vision
and a walk on the beach

Maile started this business from scratch — jobless, hopeful, and armed with a love of the ocean and a belief that the sea's discarded treasures deserved a second life. Every piece in the collection is handcrafted from materials found along local shores: sea glass worn smooth by the tide, driftwood shaped by storms, shells and stones with histories we'll never fully know.

Custom orders are always welcome — DM on Instagram or use the contact form below.
