Returns & Exchanges

Our Returns Policy

I want you to love what you receive. If something isn't right, please reach out — I'll always do my best to make it right.

What's eligible

  • ✓ Ready-to-ship pieces — accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, undamaged, and in original packaging.
  • ✓ Exchanges — always encouraged over returns. If a size or style isn't working, I'll do my best to find an alternative.
  • ✓ Damaged on arrival — full replacement or refund, no questions asked. Please photograph the damage and email me within 48 hours.
  • ✕ Made-to-order pieces — final sale. Because each MTO piece is crafted specifically for you from materials I source for your order, we cannot accept returns unless the item arrives damaged.
  • ✕ Worn or altered items — cannot be accepted. Please try on carefully over a soft surface before wearing.

Process

How to Start a Return

1

Email me first

Send a message to hello@mailittlemermaid.com with your order number and reason for the return. I personally read every message and usually reply within one business day.

2

Pack it carefully

Once we've confirmed the return, wrap the piece in its original tissue and box (or similar padding) so it doesn't shift in transit. Sea glass and shells are delicate and can chip if they're loose.

3

Ship it back

Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged. We recommend using a tracked service — I can't be responsible for items lost in return transit.

4

Refund issued

Once I receive and inspect the item I'll process your refund within 3–5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe.
